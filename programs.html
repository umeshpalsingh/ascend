INFORMATION

At Ascend Gymnastics our preschool and recreational gymnastics classes are co-ed and run on 4-week sessions.

  • Sessions are for billing purposes only.
  • You can join an open class at any point in a session for prorated tuition.
  • Each scheduled class time occurs four times per session (once per week). For example, a Monday 4:00pm class will take place each Monday within the session period.
  • You may enroll in as many classes per session as you'd like, and enrollment occurs automatically into the next session.
  • To drop a lass, the gym must be notified in writing with two weeks notice. This reserves your child's spot in the class and allows us to keep our class times and prices consistent throughout the year.
  • Classes are currently capped at 7-10 students or less, depending on the class level.
  • Trial classes are offered for free.
  • Please see our Polices for more details on how sessions work.
